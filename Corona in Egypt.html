Corona infections in Egypt

Cairo, Giza, Alexandria, and Qalyubia top the list of governorates with the highest number of cases of the new coronavirus, or what has become known as "Covid-19", according to the country's Ministry of Health.

The following is the ranking of the governorates and the rates of infection in them, according to Hala Zayed, Minister of Health and Population, during a press conference on Wednesday, and carried by Ahram Gate: Cairo 31.7%, Giza 9.6%, Alexandria 8.2%, Qalyubia 5.9%, Beheira 5.1%, Gharbia 4.0%, Fayoum 3.9%, Minya 3.7%, Menoufia 3.2%, Kafr El Sheikh 2.8%, Sohag 2.2%, Assiut 2.2%, Suez 1.9%, Sharqia 1.9%, and Beni Suef 1.7%. Aswan came 1.7%, Port Said 1.6%, Qena 1.5%, Luxor 1.3%, Dakahlia 1.2%, Damietta 1.2%, Matrouh 0.9%, Red Sea 0.7%, Ismailia 0.6%, South Sinai 0.5%, and New Valley 0.4%.

Keep yourself and others safe: Take all measures!

Protect yourself and the people around you:

  1. Keep a distance of at least one meter from others, even if they don't have symptoms. Avoid crowds and close contact.
  2. Wear a mask appropriately when you cannot implement physical distancing measures and when you are in a poorly ventilated space.
  3. Clean your hands regularly with alcohol-based hand sanitizer or wash them with soap and water.
  4. Cover your mouth and nose with the bend of your elbow or a tissue when coughing or sneezing. Discard the used tissue immediately and clean your hands regularly.
  5. If you develop symptoms of COVID-19 or test positive for COVID-19, isolate yourself from others until you recover from the disease.
